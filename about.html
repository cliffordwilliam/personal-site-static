About Me

I am a Craftsman

Hi, I am Clifford William from a small city in Indonesia called Bandung. I really like to build things. That's sums up who I am. It does not matter what I need to learn or use, any cool web app I see online would motivate me to study and spend countless hours on it using any tech stack that I need to get it working.

Why do I like to build things? I do not like to build just about anything, I like to make software / computer related things. I am unsure myself to why but I am confident to say that I think it is because of my perfectionist nature. I am not saying however that working with computers always are always organized. CSS for instance in my honest opinion is a complete mess and one must find the pattern that works in the madness. The motivation that drives me to create is simply because I like the process, and making beautiful things.

Biography

From Engineering

I graduated from engineering only to not like the work environment and culture. Everything is a mess and no one really knows what they are doing. Everything lacks certainty. At least where I work back then.

I was introduced to programming from my Manufacturing and Design professor in my Mechanical Engineering final year. I was forced into making a double pendulum simulation in MATLAB. Long story short I ended up liking MATLAB over anything else my classes had to offer back then.

Now

Fullstack trying web security

I have decided on learning fullstack development, right now I am trying to get employed in my home country. During my unemployment I would spend my time on either making more web development projects or working on my dream video game called Massive Recoil.

Personality & Hobbies

I am a reserved, quiet man who can socialize but prefers to read documentations or best practices in coding. For fun I like to play story based games and Tekken 7. I also like working in a big team, I like getting my pull requests accepted.
